A socio-economic analysis of Balkan Cinematogrophy over the 20th and 21st century

Cinematogrophy has always been an important part of Balkan culture ever since the TV became widespread in the second half of the 20th century. By analyzing the data, we will try to answer some questions regarding the cinema of this part of the world. 

When and how did it began?
What themes did the first movies have?
How have the themes changed over the years?
What economic factors have affected Balkan cinematogrophy? 
Has Balkan cinematography experienced a downfall over the years?

These questions seemes interesting to us and by trying to look at the data avaible and answer them we can maybe uncover a shift in culture. 
The datasets we will be using are:
 IMDB: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/tmdb/tmdb-movie-metadata
 Lumiere: https://lumiere.obs.coe.int/
 Kaggle: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rounakbanik/the-movies-dataset

 These are all open movie datasets thats hold all sorts of information from the year realised, the genre, the country, the spoken language and also the budget and revenue of a film.
